Physics Informed Radiomics Standardization Standardization

Date: February 3, 2025 | Author: Yijie Yuan

Developed algorithms for differentiable approximation of radiomics features such as Histogram, GLCM, and GLRLM-based features.

Background

Variability in radiomics feature extraction due to different imaging conditions is a major limitation in AI-based diagnostics.

Methods

Our approach introduces a feature loss function that stabilizes radiomics representation across varying imaging conditions.
